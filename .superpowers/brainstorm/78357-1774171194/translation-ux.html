How should the translation appear?

After you highlight a sentence, where does the English translation show up?

Ich muss morgen zum Bürgeramt gehen, um meinen Aufenthaltstitel zu verlängern.
Translation
…to extend my residence permit.

A. Floating Popover

A tooltip-like bubble appears right below the selected text. Disappears when you click elsewhere. Minimal, contextual, non-intrusive.

Ich muss morgen zum Bürgeramt gehen, um meinen Aufenthaltstitel zu verlängern.
translate
Translation
…to extend my residence permit.
✕

B. Bottom Toast Bar

A sleek bar slides up from the bottom of the viewport with the translation. Stays visible until dismissed. Doesn't interfere with reading position.

Ich muss morgen zum Bürgeramt gehen, um meinen Aufenthaltstitel zu verlängern. ↳ …to extend my residence permit.

C. Inline Annotation

The English translation appears directly below the highlighted text inline, like a subtitle. Click to dismiss. Keeps everything in reading flow.
