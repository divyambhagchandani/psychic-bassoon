Behavior Details

Trigger

  • Fires on selectionchange (300ms debounce)
  • Only for selections ≥ 15 characters
  • Skips input fields & textareas

Animation

  • Slides up from bottom (Framer Motion)
  • Slides down on dismiss
  • Content crossfades on new selection

Dismissal

  • ✕ button click
  • Click elsewhere (deselect text)
  • New selection replaces content

Performance

  • In-memory cache (useRef Map)
  • AbortController for rapid re-selections
  • Non-streaming (~50 tokens, fast)
