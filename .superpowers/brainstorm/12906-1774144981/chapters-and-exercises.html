Exercise Types (per chapter)

🎯

Multiple Choice

Primary format — mirrors LiD exam. Questions in German with 4 options.

⭐ Priority

✍️

Fill-in-the-Blank

Type the missing German word. Tests active recall, not just recognition.

🧩

Sentence Building

Drag words into correct German word order. Great for grammar practice.

🔗

Matching Pairs

Connect German terms to definitions. Good for vocab clusters.

📖

Reading Comprehension

Read a German paragraph from the story, answer questions about it.

⏱️

Blitz Round

Timed MCQ sprint — end of chapter bonus. Builds exam-day speed.
